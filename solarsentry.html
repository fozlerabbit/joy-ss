Smart Solar Infrastructure for Bangladesh’s Urban Mobility

SolarSentry solves the unauthorised EV charging crisis by using unused rooftop spaces to power authorised, clean and cost-efficient charging and battery-swapping hubs for EV autorickshaws.

Designed for Dhaka’s density, built for national scalability.

Key Features

Optimised Solar Utilisation

Rooftop solar harvesting tailored for maximum energy yield, even in congested urban zones.

Authorised Charging Stations

Ground-floor EV charging points reduce grid theft and provide regulated, stable service.

Battery Swap Network

Fast, reliable swapping ensures autorickshaws return to roads within minutes.

How SolarSentry Works

1. Rooftop Solar Installation

Unused building rooftops in Dhaka and other cities are fitted with high-efficiency solar arrays.

2. Energy Distribution

Solar energy is channelled to ground floor authorised charging stations through smart inverters.

3. Battery Charging & Storage

Dedicated rooms charge and store batteries for the swap network operated by trained teams.

4. EV Swapping & Charging

Autorickshaw drivers either charge directly or request a quick battery swap at partner hubs.

Why It Matters

Reduced Grid Pressure

Mitigates Bangladesh’s electricity theft and peak-load instability.

Lower Driver Cost

Affordable subscription or per-swap model cuts daily EV operation expenses.

Urban Sustainability

Advances national renewable energy goals under ICT Division & Sustainable Development policies.
